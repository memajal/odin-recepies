Pinwheels Recepy

Description

Pinwheels are a must-have at holiday parties. This recipe is easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
  • 2 green onions, minced
  • 4 (12 inch) flour tortillas
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup red bell pepper, diced
  • ½ cup diced celery

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mix together cream cheese, ranch dressing mix, and green onions in a medium bowl until combined.
  3. Spread onto each tortilla. Sprinkle evenly with cheese, red pepper, celery, and olives.
  4. Roll up tortillas, then wrap each one tightly in aluminum foil.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator, 2 hours to overnight.
  6. To serve, unwrap each rolled tortilla; trim ends and slice into 1-inch pieces.
